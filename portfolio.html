Case Study 03

Target Audience & Buyer Personas

Persona 1 — Fredrick

Institutional Allocator / IC Mindset

Primary Goal

Determine whether this firm deserves time, diligence, and a second meeting.

Scanning For

Decision quality, risk management, repeatable process, and signs of restraint.

Builds Trust

Clear decision frameworks, plain language about risk, ownership mindset, understatement.

Turns Him Off

Marketing language, outcome-based claims, consultant speak, values statements without operational tie-in.

Persona 2 — Robert

Sophisticated Retail / RIA-Advised Investor

Primary Goal

Feel confident that this firm is competent, disciplined, and worthy of trust.

Scanning For

Clarity of offering, steady tone, risk awareness, and language he can explain to others.

Builds Trust

Calm measured tone, long-term orientation, institutional validation, human but serious language.

Makes Him Uneasy

Aggressive language, excessive jargon, big claims without grounding, feeling sold to.

5-Second Skim Memory Test

What does Fredrick remember if interrupted?

01

You are an owner-operator, not an allocator or advisor. "They actually own and operate."

02

You care deeply about downside and discipline. "Conservative at entry. Downside, basis, durability."

03

You've built a system, not a story. "They've thought through how decisions get made."

04

You've operated at scale. "This isn't a first-time shop."

05

You pay attention to residents. "Close to demand and what's happening on the ground."

Brand Strategy

Website Landing Page Redesign

Challenge

The existing website was outdated, repetitive, and failed to clearly differentiate the firm. Leadership needed a refreshed, strategy-led landing page that better communicated the firm's approach and resonated with prospective investors.

Approach

Conducted buyer persona research to clarify audience needs and priorities, then developed the full content strategy and narrative structure. Wrote all copy and designed the page experience end-to-end. Built the site in Squarespace, using custom code via Cursor to achieve the intended layout and functionality. Converted the final product into a portfolio-ready wireframe using AI tools to preserve structure while protecting live content.

Tools & Deliverables

Squarespace Cursor Buyer Persona Research Content Strategy Copywriting UX Layout Design

Outcome

Project currently in progress. The redesign addresses long-standing gaps in differentiation and content quality — outcomes to be measured upon launch.

Investor Communications

After Action Review Report

Challenge

Investors needed clear visibility into where their fund scores stood and a concrete path to improving them. The information existed but lacked a structured, visual format that made it easy to understand and act on.

Approach

Developed the report template from scratch by first sitting down with the department lead to understand the scoring methodology and stakeholder needs. Tools & Deliverables

Adobe InDesign Adobe Illustrator Template Design Data Visualization Investor Communications

Outcome

Became a recurring deliverable within the investor communications cadence. Increased transparency into GRESB reporting and scoring — giving investors a clearer picture of fund performance and a defined roadmap for continued improvement.
